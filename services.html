Basement Remodeling

❮ ❯

Often overlooked and neglected, a typical basement takes up more than 40% of your home’s overall space. Which is why it’s one of the most important parts in your home to look great and be fully functional. Whether you want to enjoy it as a full experience entertainment room, or eventually rent it out for rental income, a finished basement will surely increase the overall value of any property.

Kitchen Remodeling

❮ ❯

It’s the heart and soul of any home. The center of food preparation and hospitality, it should be the most inviting room in the house. Turning a lacklustre afterthought of a kitchen into a modern, open concept gathering space showcases your design flair and personality without having to compromise on functionality. With clever use of counter space, cupboard space, as well as ample flooring, your kitchen can accommodate all of your entertainment and personal needs. Afterall, the party always ends up in the kitchen.

Bathroom Remodeling

❮ ❯

A newly renovated bathroom can create a statement piece, or a calming sanctuary. From flooring to showers, and sinks to tubs, our construction experts will work alongside you to create a design plan with your existing space and budget.

Patio/Deck/Fence
